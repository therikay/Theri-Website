Theri Reichlin

Urban Forester and coder based in Europe

Theri Reichlin has a diverse background, with Czech and Swiss heritage but also heavily influenced by Canadian and Spanish culture. Her exposure to rural life and life in large cities led her to pursue a career in urban forestry with a focus on community engagement. She is passionate about the role of gender equality in environmental conservation. As of recently she is also a keen learner of coding, currently undergoing training by SheCodes Workshops. Her vision for the future involves trees in cities, tech and maybe writing a childrens book about foresters.
